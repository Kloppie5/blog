The ITOTW numbers refer to Igor's tip of the week, a weekly blog post by Hex-Rays.
On the pages indicated by the ITOTW numbers, there are hints in the form of span tags with the class corresponding to the hint.
This gives us the parts ("ID"), ("en"), ("A", "ll"), ("we"), ("o", "-"), ("2", "0") and ("22").
These pages could have also been found by looking at the modification dates in the sitemap.xml file.
I grouped the letters per part, and made the key "IDAllo-ween2022", which seemed correct.
The blog post mentions "Have you found all the missing parts? They will help you to find the hidden page.", but I was initially unable to find a page with the key.

The next morning, I was looking into this again when I got hit by a terrible feeling.
I looked at the individual parts and created "IDAlloween-2022", ignoring the grouping per page.
I hoped that this was not correct, but it was.
The page "hex-rays.com/IDAlloween-2022/" gives the instructions to complete the challenge (mailing the marketing team with the subject "IDAloween2022").

It might have taken a bit longer than it should have, but at least I got there in the end.
